### **Revenue Insight in Hospitality Domain**



##### 1\.**Project Overview:**

This project focuses on analyzing hotel revenue data to provide meaningful business insights for the hospitality industry.  

The dashboard helps management track performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.



##### 2**.Business Problem:**

A luxury hotel chain was facing declining revenue and market share.  

Management needed a clear and interactive dashboard to understand booking behavior, occupancy, and revenue performance.



##### 3\.**Tools Used:**

\- Power BI

\- DAX

\- Power Query

\- Data Modeling

\- Data Visualization



##### 4\.**Key Metrics:**

\- Total Revenue

\- Occupancy Rate

\- Average Daily Rate (ADR)

\- Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR)

\- Booking and Cancellation Analysis



##### 5**.Dashboard Features:**

\- Interactive filters and slicers

\- City-wise and room-wise performance analysis

\- Weekday vs Weekend comparison

\- Q\&A visual for asking business questions

\- Guided information buttons for easy understanding



##### **6.Dataset:**

The dataset includes booking details, hotel information, room types, and date dimensions.



##### **7.Outcome:**

The dashboard provides actionable insights that help improve pricing strategy, occupancy planning, and revenue optimization.
